Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I access premium stories?
A: Subscribe to our premium plan through the app. Go to Settings > Premium to see subscription options.
Q: Why can't I hear audio pronunciation?
A: Make sure your device is not in silent mode, volume is turned up and you have a stable internet connection. Double-tap any French sentence to hear pronunciation.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device, find FrenchQ, and select Cancel Subscription.
Q: The app crashes or won't load. What should I do?
A: Try restarting the app or your device. Make sure you have the latest version installed from the App Store.
Q: Can I use the app offline?
A: Some content is available offline after initial download.

Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical problems:

  1. Ensure you have the latest app version
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Restart the app
  4. Restart your device
  5. Contact us if the problem persists
